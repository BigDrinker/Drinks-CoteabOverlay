Coteab Native Overlay 2.2 - Stable Final Pass

Included:
- Stable native per-pixel overlay
- Background-only transparency with opaque text
- Rounded transparent corners
- Move/resize and saved bounds
- Status, session, biome, aura, rarity, merchant, and merchant timer
- Profiles, row ordering, compact/expanded layouts
- Detection notifications and session counters
- Import/export overlay settings
- Automatic retention of the five most recent settings backups
- Compiled-mode overlay launcher
- One-click windowed EXE build script

Deliberately not included in this stability pass:
- FPS/ping estimates
- Audio alerts
- animated GPU effects
- global hotkeys
- potion timers without a verified macro data source

Those features can introduce hooks, timing races, or misleading values and were
left out of the final stability-focused build.
